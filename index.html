We combine 30 years of collective experience in engineering and distribution-line construction. Our mission is to deliver top-tier, innovative solutions—setting new benchmarks in quality and customer satisfaction throughout the industry.

About Us

Kilovolt Engineering, founded in 2021 and headquartered in Warner Robins, Georgia, specializes in all facets of distribution engineering up to 25 kV. What sets us apart is our hands-on field experience and deep understanding of real-world electrical challenges.

Our founders bring together an exceptional 30 years of expertise in the power industry, focusing on MV planning, troubleshooting, protective-device coordination, sectionalizing studies, and field inspections.

We have successfully completed several USDA RUS work plans for rural utilities—most recently a plan valued at ≈ $100 million—and manage engineering operations for fleets of over 50 substations and 200 feeder circuits.

Our mission is to provide engineering solutions that stand up to the highest industry standards for quality and reliability.

Our Services

Inspection & Field Services

  • Meticulous USDA RUS Form 219 work-order inspections verified by P.E.
  • Power-line RFI locating, stray-voltage investigations, and surge-arrester diagnostics
  • Pole-ground & service-ground resistance testing

Distribution Engineering, Planning & Protection Services

  • Load-flow, short-circuit, and voltage-profile simulations in WindMil
  • Conductor, transformer, and equipment sizing, plus full USDA RUS technical work plans
  • Protection coordination (351S, 651-R, fuses, TripSavers, OCRs, reclosers, etc.)
  • Distribution arc-flash studies to ensure proper calorie arc-rated clothing
  • EV fast-charging infrastructure design
  • Outage-data analytics (SAIDI/SAIFI dashboards) using custom VBA & SQL code
  • NEC/NESC code interpretation
  • Photometric lighting studies for roadways and parking lots, light placement and light selection

SCADA Services

  • RTAC & gateway programming, DNP3 integration
  • Rotational load-shed (RLS) algorithms to meet utility curtailment mandates
  • Custom Survalent HMI dashboards
  • Survalent design and troubleshooting
  • Firmware lifecycle management and operator training

Representative Projects

  • 41.7 MVA 115 kV/12.47 kV Substation (2023). Lead protection & SCADA commissioning—including permissive, mirrored-bits scheme to prevent double-circuit miscoordination.
  • System-wide Distribution Automation Deployment (2021-24). Programmed & commissioned 240 + reclosers and 150 + breaker relays under an RTAC-driven centralized controller, boosting reliability KPIs by double-digit percentages.
  • EV Fast-Charging Infrastructure Design. Sized medium-voltage infrastructure such as transformers and conductors, coordinated logistics with Tesla and Mercedes-Benz for an 8 MVA (peak load) EV charging hub in middle Georgia.
  • Rotational Load-Shed Integration. Implemented Survalent RLS module enabling automated distibution circuit curtailments in 15-minute rotations—compliant with Southern Power Balancing Authority directives.
  • Feeder Reliability Analytics. Built VBA/SQL tool that converts raw OMS data into SAIDI/SAIFI heat-maps, guiding CAPEX toward the worst-performing circuits and saving manual analysis hours every month. Selected locations for reclosers in the worst-performing areas to prevent expensive truck rolls.
