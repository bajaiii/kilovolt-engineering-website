We combine 30 years of collective experience in engineering and distribution-line construction. Our mission is to deliver top-tier, innovative solutions—setting new benchmarks in quality and customer satisfaction throughout the industry.

About Us

Kilovolt Engineering, founded in 2021 and headquartered in Warner Robins, Georgia, specializes in all facets of distribution engineering up to 25 kV. What sets us apart is our hands-on field experience and deep understanding of real-world electrical challenges.

Our founders bring together an exceptional 30 years of expertise in the power industry, focusing on MV planning, troubleshooting, protective-device coordination, sectionalizing studies, and field inspections.

We have successfully completed several USDA RUS work plans for rural utilities—most recently a plan valued at ≈ $100 million—and manage engineering operations for fleets of over 50 substations and 200 feeder circuits.

Our mission is to provide engineering solutions that stand up to the highest industry standards for quality and reliability.

Our Services

Inspection & Field Services

  • Meticulous RUS work-order inspection reports verified by Professional Engineer (P.E.) in several states
  • Thorough pad-mount and underground infrastructure inspections and write-ups
  • Comprehensive asset inventory assessments
  • Quick-response storm-damage analysis
  • Power-Line Radio Frequency Interference (RFI) locating and reports
  • Troubleshooting—With over 15 years of service-truck and construction-truck experience up to 25 kV, we excel in solving complex electrical problems
  • Pole-ground & electrical-service-ground resistance measurements

Distribution Engineering Analysis & Planning Services

  • Intricate system modeling and simulations
  • Conductor, transformer, and equipment sizing
  • Completion of RUS technical work plans
  • Power-factor fine-tuning and analysis—capacitor sizing to improve power factor
  • Specialized in distribution feeder/circuit coordination up to 25 kV
  • Proficiency in Schweitzer relay settings (351S and 651R), fuse sizing, and oil-circuit-recloser (OCR) sizing and curve selection
  • Expertise in Hubbell Versa-Tech settings, TripSaver configurations, and VWVE parameters
  • NEC & NESC code interpretation and research
  • Professional Engineering (P.E.) services and stamped documentation
  • Creation of Python or Excel VBA models to analyze historical outage data from OMS SQL databases
  • Development of Distribution Specifications
  • Parking-lot, roadway, and substation lighting studies with photometric design

SCADA Services

  • Design and configuration of SCADA RTUs/RTACs and communication networks
  • Commissioning new Intelligent Electronic Devices (IEDs)
  • Integration of new substations into existing SCADA systems
  • SCADA HMI design and custom dashboard development for real-time monitoring
  • Telemetry installation and testing (DNP3, IEC 61850, Modbus)
  • Firmware updates for RTUs/RTACs
  • Training and technical support for operations staff on SCADA platforms
